Single to Multisite

This is an automatic tool allowing to transform site from single one into multisite and back. In addition, it can make a complete copy of one of your multisite domains to create a new sub-domain (or sub-path).

Before you launch any operation it is strongly advised to make a complete backup. Even though all operations are totally fail-safe to run, nobody canceled force-major situations (as unexpected server or electricity shutdown during operation).
